ABOUT

HSIAH「夏」

Founded on the intersection of Eastern heritage and contemporary streetwear, HSIAH (夏) draws from the raw energy of summer — a season of transformation, rebellion, and unbridled expression.

Each collection is a chapter in an ongoing narrative that bridges traditional Chinese aesthetics with the pulse of modern youth culture. From the dream-laden escapism of 夢棄少年 to the visceral intensity of TARANTELLA, every piece is designed to provoke, to question, and to wear like armor.

HSIAH is not just clothing. It is a language spoken through fabric, cut, and intention.

01 — HERITAGE

Rooted in Eastern philosophy and visual traditions, reinterpreted for the now.

02 — CRAFT

Every garment is meticulously constructed with premium materials and considered details.

03 — NARRATIVE

Collections tell stories. Each season unfolds a new chapter in the HSIAH universe.
